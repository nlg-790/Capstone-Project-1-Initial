Different Types of Diets

Explore various diet plans and find out which one might be right for you:

Vegan Diet

A vegan diet excludes all animal products, including meat, dairy, and eggs. It focuses on plant-based foods such as fruits, vegetables, grains, nuts, and seeds.

Ketogenic Diet

The ketogenic diet is a high-fat, low-carbohydrate diet that aims to burn fat more efficiently by forcing the body into a state of ketosis.

Paleo Diet

This diet is designed to resemble what human hunter-gatherer ancestors ate thousands of years ago. It includes lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds and excludes processed foods and grains.

Mediterranean Diet

Inspired by the eating habits of Greece and Italy, the Mediterranean diet emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish, and moderate amounts of red wine.

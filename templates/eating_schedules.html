Eating Schedules and Patterns

Learn about different eating schedules and how they can impact your health and wellness:

Intermittent Fasting

Intermittent fasting cycles between periods of fasting and eating. Common patterns include the 16/8 method, where you fast for 16 hours and eat during an 8-hour window, and the 5:2 method, where you eat normally for 5 days a week and restrict calories for 2 non-consecutive days.

6 Small Meals a Day

Instead of three large meals, some prefer eating smaller, more frequent meals throughout the day to manage hunger and blood sugar levels.

Traditional Three-Meal Structure

The conventional breakfast, lunch, and dinner structure has long been followed for its simplicity and alignment with a typical workday.
